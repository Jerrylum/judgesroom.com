# Judging Guidelines Change Summary

## Required Awards

- **Excellence Award**, **Design Award**, and **Judges Award** are required awards if judging occurs at an event.
- Other awards remain available.

## Award Categories

- Volunteer-nominated awards are now called Nominated Awards.

## Nominated Awards

- **Sportsmanship Award** and **Energy Award** are Nominated Awards only.
- Sportsmanship and Energy can no longer be treated as Judged Awards.
- Both event volunteers and judges can nominate teams for Nominated Awards, while Nominated Awards still remain outside the main Judged Award deliberation flow.

## Judged Award Precedence

When resolving judged-award conflicts or applying a one-judged-award-per-team flow, use this order:

1. Excellence Award
2. Design Award
3. Innovate Award
4. Think Award
5. Amaze Award
6. Build Award
7. Create Award
8. Judges Award
9. Inspire Award

VIQRC events now use the same judged-award precedence as other programs. **Create Award no longer has higher precedence in VIQRC events** and now follows Build Award.

## Design Award

- At certain blended events, separate Design Awards may be presented by grade level as defined by the Event Qualification Guide.

## Innovate Award

- The Innovate Award Submission Form is no longer required for Innovate Award eligibility.

## Engineering Notebook

- All notebooks must be evaluated using the Engineering Notebook Rubric, not only Fully Developed notebooks.
- Only Fully Developed notebooks are eligible for Design, Innovate, and Excellence awards.
- A new Engineering Notebook Rubric is available.

## Team Interview

- A new Team Interview Rubric is available.
